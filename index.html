Research Experience

My research was mainly focused on data acquisition from university attendees (their status updates) and predicting three types of bad personality traits we see on social media. I automated the data fetching phase of my research by developing an android application that used facebook’s graph API. The app worked in tandem with scripts written in google scripts to generate datasets that are needed to train several supervised machine learning algorithms. We also included potential phrases from the data via “Phrasemachine” written in R in our data pool. The data was preprocessed via MATLAB. After our research came to a conclusion, we got better accuracy than the majority class baseline.
